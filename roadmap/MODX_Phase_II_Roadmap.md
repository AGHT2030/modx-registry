# MODX Phase II Roadmap

Phase II introduces expanded access and governance infrastructure.

---

## Inclusions
- Broker Gateway
- Investor Portal
- Philanthropic Routing
- Participant Onboarding Administrator (POA)
- AURA Sentinel (Advisory Only)

---

## Exclusions
- No secondary markets
- No evergreen capital contracts
- No outcome-based fees
- No permanent project retention

---

## Objective

Enable fair, time-bound access while preserving project sovereignty.
