# MODX Definitions & Glossary

This glossary defines roles and terms used across the MODX Sovereign Technologies platform.  
All definitions are binding and immutable for the duration of the platform version in which they are published.

---

## Participant Onboarding Administrator (POA)

An administrative partner responsible solely for participant education, intake completeness verification, and eligibility processing at Level 0.

The POA:
- Has no authority over allocation, timing, project selection, or outcomes
- Does not provide investment advice
- Does not influence level advancement beyond mechanical rule fulfillment
- Does not access non-public opportunity data
- Does not receive outcome-based or project-specific compensation

---

## Broker

A third-party introducer that may refer participants to the MODX platform.
Brokers have no authority over allocation, sequencing, pricing, or outcomes.

---

## Participant

An individual or entity that has completed onboarding and participates in the MODX platform under defined level rules.

---

## Maturity

The stage at which a project has achieved its declared development milestones and exits the MODX platform.

---

## Sovereign Release

The formal delisting of a mature project from MODX governance, oversight, and association.
