# Platform Scope Limitations

MODX Sovereign Technologies operates as an allocation governance and lifecycle platform.

---

## 1. What MODX Is

MODX provides:
- Fair access frameworks
- Allocation governance
- Time-bound participation
- Project lifecycle enforcement

---

## 2. What MODX Is Not

MODX does not:
- Operate secondary markets
- Provide investment advice
- Guarantee outcomes
- Act as a broker-dealer
- Retain projects beyond maturity

---

## 3. Independent Decision Making

All participants:
- Act independently
- Make their own decisions
- Bear responsibility for their choices

MODX does not direct or compel participation.
